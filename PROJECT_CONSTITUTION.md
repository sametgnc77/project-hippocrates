# Project Hippocrates Constitution

## Article 1

We are building an AI-powered personal learning operating system, not a conventional study application.

---

## Article 2

Learning quality is always more important than feature quantity.

---

## Article 3

Every piece of data entering the system must improve at least two other modules.

(Zero Data Waste Principle)

---

## Article 4

Every feature must measurably improve learning efficiency or TUS performance.

If it does not, it will not be developed.

---

## Article 5

Artificial Intelligence is an assistant, not the source of truth.

Every critical output must remain traceable to its original source.

---

## Article 6

The architecture must remain modular.

Every module should have a single responsibility and communicate with other modules through well-defined interfaces.

---

## Article 7

The system is designed for continuous evolution.

Nothing is static.

The user learns.

The AI learns.

The system improves.

---

## Article 8

The project values long-term maintainability over short-term speed.

---

## Article 9

The system exists to create a digital learning twin of the user.

---

## Article 10

Code follows architecture.

Architecture follows documentation.

Documentation follows vision.
